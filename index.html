About

Nordic Combat Sports at Its Finest

ICE CAGE FIGHTING is a Finnish combat sports promotion company that brings together top-level mixed martial arts, boxing, Thai boxing, and kickboxing matches. Since our first event in December 2023, we have offered the public high-level and unforgettable combat sports evenings, where the atmosphere and intensity of the matches are guaranteed.

We combine combat sports, spectacular show elements, and high-quality production into an exciting package that offers the audience more than just fights. We create events where entertainment and top-level sports complement each other. Interest in our events is strong — all events have been sold out, with capacities of up to 9,000 spectators.

ICE CAGE is more than a fight night — it is a movement. Join us as we continue to build Finland's most exciting combat sports event and bring the heat to the biggest arenas in the Nordic region.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ICE CAGE FIGHTING? +
We are a Finnish combat sports promotion company that brings together top-level mixed martial arts, boxing, Thai boxing, and kickboxing matches. Since our first event in December 2023, we have offered the public high-level and unforgettable combat sports evenings, where the atmosphere and intensity of the matches are guaranteed.
What makes ICE CAGE events special? +
We combine combat sports, spectacular show elements, and high-quality production into an exciting package that offers the audience more than just fights. We create events where entertainment and top-level sports complement each other. All events have been sold out, with capacities of up to 9,000 spectators.
What sports and matches can you expect? +
We combine professional fights in MMA, boxing, kickboxing, and Thai boxing under the same event, building diverse and entertaining fight cards featuring the best fighters from different combat sports.
Is there a live stream available? +
Yes. We offer live streaming of all our events with top-notch camera production that conveys the intensity of the matches and the atmosphere of the event to home viewers. Live streaming is designed for those who cannot attend in person but want to experience the event in real time.
Who can attend ICE CAGE events? +
Our event is aimed at audiences of all ages. You can come alone, with your family, or with a group of friends — we offer an experience for children, young people, and adults alike. For companies, groups, and VIP audiences, we also offer VIP boxes and Premium Lounge packages.
